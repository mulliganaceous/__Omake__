Split/Merge

The Split and Merge commands allow you to alter the relationships of existing objects by splitting points from their parents, by merging points either with other points or onto paths, and by merging several text objects into one. These commands allow you to fix construction mistakes, to make significant changes in a sketch without starting over, and to modify geometric and mathematical investigations in flexible and powerful ways.
