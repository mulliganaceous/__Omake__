Angle Bisector

Selection prerequisites: Three points, with the vertex point selected second.

Constructs a ray that bisects the minor angle formed by the three selected points. The second selected point designates the vertex of the angle. For instance, to bisect angle ABC, select points in the order A, then B, and finally C.
