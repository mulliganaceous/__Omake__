If You've Used Version 3

Although Sketchpad Version 4 contains many changes and new features, users of Version 3 won't find the transition difficult. The following sections describe the changes, list the new features, and explain how to reuse your Version 3 sketches and scripts. You can also browse the sample documents installed with Sketchpad for demonstrations of many of the new version's capabilities. In particular, the document named HowTo_UpgradeFromV3 demonstrates many of the more important new features.
