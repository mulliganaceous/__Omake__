Motion Speed

You can use the Motion Controller, the Increase/Decrease Speed commands, or an Animation button to set the speed of an animating object relative to the normal speed for that object.

You can set the ideal speed for points using Advanced Preferences.

The Motion Controller displays normal speed as speed 1.0. The actual velocity of normal speed depends both on your System Preferences and on your computer. Sketchpad attempts to keep this speed constant, but if your sketch is complex or your computer is busy with other tasks, normal speed may be slightly slower than requested in System Preferences.
