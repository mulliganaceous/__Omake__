Web-Based Technical Support

A variety of technical support options are available through Spectrum or at the Key Curriculum Press Sketchpad Resource Center.

Latest Product Updates: http://www.spectrumed.com

Technical Support Requests: customerservice@spectrumed.com

Frequently Asked Questions: http://www.keypress.com/sketchpad/sketchtechsupp.html

When you e-mail technical support, in addition to your request, please identify the type of CPU and operating system on which you use Sketchpad, and the full version number of the edition of Sketchpad you’re using. (The full version number--for example, 4.00--can be located in the program's About Sketchpad dialog box.)

Telephone and Mail Technical Support

Licensed Ontario customers may also access telephone-based or mail-based technical support. If you call, please do so from a location where you can access your computer and Sketchpad.
